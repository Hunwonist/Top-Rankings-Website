Top Rankings

Top Rankings list some interesting rankings from Wikipedia https://www.wikipedia.org/, The World Bank https://data.worldbank.org/, and OECD data https://data.oecd.org/.

It provides a convenient way to visualize a ranking with animation and pictures. Also, the dynamic rankings gives an interactive way for users to see the changing of rankings in a given range of time.

The available static rankings are:

  1. Top 18 highest mountains on the earth
  2. Top 7 largest planets in the solar system
  3. Top 8 largest stars in the universe
  4. Top 10 highest bridges on the earth (2019)
  5. Top 10 heaviest land mammals on the earth
  6. Top 10 highest buildings on the earth (2019)

The available dynamic rankings are:

  1. Top 10 countries by GDP in the world (1960-2018)
  2. Top 10 countries by population in the world (1960-2018)
  3. Top 10 countries by average wage in the world (1990-2018)
  4. Top 10 countries by life expectancy in the world (1980-2017)
  5. Top 10 countries by COâ‚‚ emissions in the world (1992-2014)
  6. Top 10 countries by military expenditure in the world (1993-2018)

We will add more rankings in the future.
